Our Story

Contrary to the 'joke' Dan likes to share, we did not meet on Craigslist. We met on TheLeague, a dating app in San Francisco. Dan was combing the bay area, hoping to secure a girlfriend by Q2 of 2016. Tori was simply hoping to keep her spot in TheLeague for when she was good ready and simply swiped right to say active, a courtesy swipe if you will. Low and behold, ski banter ensued and we agreed to meet.

Dan journeyed from the land of Sperrys and frat stars (the Marina) all the way to the Haight for a first date. A second date followed fast, tennis in the Marina where Tori probably whooped Dan's butt. It was a full month before our third date as Tori spent a month in Vietnam and Dan was riding motorcyles in Utah. Dan was prudent in his pursuit, sending novel updates daily.

Finally, back in SF, the third, fourth and fifth date flew by within a few days. After watching the Blue Angels at SF Fleet week, Dan wasted NO time and asked Tori to be his girlfriend. The next day Dan was ready and waiting to run through '36 Questions that Lead to Love' from the NYT. Dan has been leading us to love ever since and we can't wait to get married.
